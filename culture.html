Culture and Heritage

Holiday leave over Christmas and the New Year is fairly long, usually extending a week into January. Efter Christmas Eve is over, swedes visits their friends and relatives and spends cozy winter days having fun. Swedes often bake and cook variety of foods during Christmas and New year.






Quality Life at Sverige


Every person in sweden has the right to live quality live. Quality life means a safe and caring environment for the child to grow and reach their full potential, swedish family system are build such way over decades. There are communities and organizations to provide a loving home for elders who need alternative care.
